About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Department of Management Science and Engineering at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), supervised by Prof. Luning Liu（刘鲁宁）.

My research research interests primarily focus on two areas: (1) Visual persuasion strategies and mechanisms in digital platforms; (2) Online entrepreneurial fundraising (crowdfunding)

Methodologically, I employ a mixed-methods approach combining Econometrics, Machine Learning, Text Analysis, and Experiments to derive actionable empirical insights from complex digital platform data.
